# Commercial: Recruitment Applications

The recruitment vertical bridges the gap between job seekers, employment agencies, and hiring organizations. A Solid Databox fundamentally changes this ecosystem by allowing candidates to manage a secure, cryptographically verified portfolio of their professional lives, eliminating the need to repeatedly fill out background checks and disparate application forms.

## Core Candidate Data Payloads

By leveraging a Solid Databox, the recruitment process shifts from self-asserted (and often unverified) claims on a standard CV, to a trust-centric model built entirely on W3C Verifiable Credentials (VCs).

### 1. Professional History & Roles
Candidates securely store and share a verified, chronological history of their employment:
- **Verified Roles & Titles:** Cryptographic proofs of employment issued directly by former employers, detailing exact job titles, dates of tenure, and primary responsibilities. This provides absolute certainty regarding a candidate's work history.
- **Performance & Endorsements:** Official letters of recommendation, performance milestones, or internal awards issued as Verifiable Credentials, actively combating resume fraud.

### 2. Comprehensive Credentials
The databox serves as a unified digital wallet for all prerequisites required for employment across various sectors:
- **Academic Credentials:** Comprehensive Learner Records (CLR) or Open Badges v3.0 detailing university degrees, vocational certificates, and high-school diplomas natively exported from educational institutions.
- **Professional Licenses:** Active verifications for regulated industries (e.g., Nursing registrations, CPA licenses, Bar association memberships, Trade certifications) that automatically update or flag if they expire or are revoked.
- **Clearances & Background Checks:** Verified claims from government or authorized third-party agencies, such as Security Clearances, Working With Children Checks (WWCC), or National Police Certificates. This allows for instant verification, saving candidates from undergoing redundant, weeks-long background checks for every new application.

## The Solid Databox Workflow

1. **Consent & Application:** The candidate applies for a role by granting the recruiter or hiring platform scoped read-access to specific slices of their Solid Pod (e.g., only granting access to their academic records and police clearance, while keeping their full work history private).
2. **Instant Verification:** The recruitment Applicant Tracking System (ATS) ingests the Verifiable Credentials. Because they are cryptographically signed by the issuing authorities (universities, past employers, government agencies), the recruiter instantly trusts the data without needing to run manual, third-party background checks.
3. **Data Minimization & Revocation:** Once the hiring process concludes, the candidate can instantly revoke access to their highly sensitive data (such as their background checks or government IDs), adhering to strict data minimization and personal privacy principles.
