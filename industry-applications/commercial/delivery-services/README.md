# Commercial: Delivery Services & Reputation Management

The "gig economy" and delivery sector is currently dominated by centralized platforms that extract exorbitant fees and monopolize customer data. Integrating Databoxes enables a decentralized delivery ecosystem where local delivery providers establish direct, secure relationships with their customers. Furthermore, the Databox issues cryptographically verifiable credentials for drivers (e.g., proof of insurance, background checks, or consistent delivery times). This allows independent delivery providers to prove their assurance and manage a portable, un-censorable reputation across the entire ecosystem, completely freeing them from the lock-in of traditional aggregator apps.
