# Sporting Organisations Applications

Sporting bodies—from grassroots local clubs to elite national leagues—manage an enormous amount of personal, medical, and performance data. Currently, this data is siloed within individual clubs, meaning a player's history is often lost when they move teams. 

Integrating a Solid Databox transforms this ecosystem, empowering athletes to maintain a portable, sovereign **"Athletic Passport"** that moves seamlessly with them across clubs, leagues, and medical professionals.

## Core Athletic Data Payloads

By leveraging a Solid Databox, the sporting ecosystem shifts from fragmented club databases to a unified, player-centric data model built on Verifiable Credentials (VCs).

### 1. Registration, Identity, & Memberships
- **Verified Player Identity & Age:** Clubs strictly require proof of age to enforce division rules (e.g., Under-18s leagues). Players can grant read-access to a verified identity VC to prove eligibility instantly, without local clubs maintaining massive databases of vulnerable birth certificates or passports.
- **Transfers & Clearances:** When a player changes clubs, a "Transfer Clearance" VC is deposited into their Databox by the exiting club. The player instantly presents this to the new club, automating the historically slow and bureaucratic clearance process.
- **Coaching & Volunteering Credentials:** Coaches, referees, and volunteers store their accreditations (e.g., First Aid, Coaching Licenses, Working With Children Checks) in their Databox. The league can query these before a match to mathematically verify that all staff on the field are legally compliant.

### 2. Medical, Concussion, & Welfare Records
Protecting player welfare requires instant access to accurate medical history across different teams and leagues.
- **Concussion Protocols & Medical Clearance:** If an athlete suffers a concussion, the attending medical professional issues a time-stamped "Stand Down" VC to the player's Pod. If the player attempts to play for a different team (e.g., their school team vs. their weekend club) the next day, the system will flag the active concussion protocol. Once neurologically cleared, a "Return to Play" VC is issued.
- **Allergies & Medical Flags:** Players maintain a verified medical profile (e.g., asthma plans, severe allergies) that club medics can securely query on game day to ensure rapid, appropriate emergency response.

### 3. Performance Data & League Tables
- **Verified Statistics:** Rather than statistics being locked entirely in a proprietary league database, official match outcomes, goals scored, and personal bests are issued as VCs directly to the player's Databox after every game.
- **Decentralized League Tables:** Leagues can aggregate anonymized or explicitly consented performance VCs directly from player and club pods. This allows the construction of dynamic, cryptographically verifiable league tables and leaderboards, ensuring absolute integrity of the competition results and preventing data manipulation.
- **The Athletic Portfolio (Scouting):** Players aggregate these statistical VCs over their career. When seeking to join an elite academy, a representative team, or a college sports program, the athlete shares a cryptographically verified portfolio of their performance history, completely immune to exaggeration or resume fraud.

## The Solid Databox Workflow

1. **Season Registration:** The athlete logs into the league portal and grants temporary read-access to their identity, age, and medical VCs to complete registration instantly and securely.
2. **Game Day Safety:** Team medics are granted localized read-access to critical health flags (like asthma action plans) for the duration of the match.
3. **Post-Match Updates:** The league issues cryptographically signed performance VCs (e.g., "Player of the Match", "Match Result", "Goals Scored") directly into the athlete's Databox. This automatically updates the decentralized league tables.
4. **Career Progression:** The athlete builds a lifelong, verifiable digital portfolio of their sporting achievements that remains completely under their control, irrespective of which clubs or leagues they participate in over their lifetime.
