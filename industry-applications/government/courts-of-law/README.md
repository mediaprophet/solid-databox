# Courts of Law Applications

Courts of Law can leverage the Databox architecture to transform how highly sensitive legal documents are distributed and managed. This includes the cryptographically secure delivery of court orders, verifiable claims, electronic subpoenas, bail conditions, and restraining orders directly to a citizen's databox. It also enables citizens or legal representatives to securely submit immutable evidentiary records and compliance receipts, ensuring chain-of-custody and complete auditability without relying on insecure email or paper trails.

## Structured Considerations
- Supporting transparent and secure access to lawful remedy.
- Digital evidence management and provenance.
- Interoperability with justice and law-enforcement portals.
