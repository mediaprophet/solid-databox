# Components: Micro-Credentials & "Tickets"

Traditional degrees and long-form certifications are no longer sufficient to capture the agility required in modern workforces and specialized trades. The Solid Databox provides a robust infrastructure for issuing, storing, and instantly verifying highly granular micro-credentials.

## Core Mechanisms & Standards

- **Open Badges v3.0 & W3C VCs:** Micro-credentials within this ecosystem are fully aligned with the W3C Verifiable Credentials data model (frequently implementing the 1EdTech Open Badges v3.0 specification). This ensures they are universally recognized, mathematically verifiable, and entirely portable across different employment sectors.
- **Granular Skill Representation:** Unlike a monolithic university transcript, micro-credentials represent specific, atomic skills. This allows a worker to curate a hyper-specific, stackable portfolio of their capabilities over their lifetime.

## Key Use Cases

### 1. Trade "Tickets" & Industrial Safety
- **High-Risk Work Licenses:** In the construction, mining, and industrial sectors, workers must constantly prove they hold the correct "tickets" (e.g., Forklift operation, Confined Space Entry, Working at Heights, Asbestos removal).
- **Instant Site Access:** By storing these micro-credentials in the Databox, a worker can simply tap their smartphone at a construction site turnstile. The site system instantly verifies the cryptographic signature of the ticket and ensures it hasn't expired or been revoked, drastically improving site safety and eliminating the administrative overhead of manual safety checks.

### 2. Education & Continuous Professional Development (CPD)
- **Short Courses & Bootcamps:** Universities, TAFEs, and private academies issue micro-credentials upon the successful completion of intensive short courses, coding bootcamps, or specialized executive training.
- **CPD Tracking:** For heavily regulated professions (e.g., Nursing, Accounting, Law), professionals can aggregate micro-credentials representing their mandatory ongoing training hours, providing instant, immutable proof of compliance to their regulatory bodies.
