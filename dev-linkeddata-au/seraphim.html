Seraphim

A consumer-controlled Solid client Flutter application.

Launch Web App

Data Architecture

Following the architectural pattern of the ANU Software Innovation Institute (anusii), this application relies entirely on a remote, decentralized "online-first" model. It reads and writes encrypted data directly to your Solid Pod via solidpod and solidui, keeping your structured data securely in user-controlled remote vaults.

Human Rights & Qualia-DB

Future implementations of this application intended to support human rights will be backed by a qualia-db license for natural persons. This ensures the protection and sovereignty of the individual's data across the semantic web.

Features

  • Domain Driven Design: 15 built-in functional domains including Case Plans, Budgets, and Evidence.
  • Hardware Ready: Scan check-in QR codes to resolve Solid Endpoints or snap photos of receipts for direct Pod upload.
  • Verifiable Presentations: Present credentials stored in your Pod dynamically as QR codes.
